PART B :- Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:

Create a simple checklist (can be a table or a bulleted list in your report) with at least 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student.





✔️ Do’s



•Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate. 

•Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information. 

•Share accurate information and verify facts before posting or forwarding anything. 

•Maintain a professional online image because future employers may view your profile. 

•Respect others online by using polite language and avoiding arguments or offensive comments. 

❌ Don’ts



* Don't share personal information such as phone number, address, passwords, or bank details.
* Don’t post offensive, harmful, or abusive content that could hurt others.
* Don’t spread rumors or fake news without verifying the source.
* Don’t overshare private moments that could affect your reputation.
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or respond aggressively to negative comments.
